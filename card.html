Lion

Lion is the king of animals

  • Family: Felidae
  • Genus: Panthera
  • Inhabitance: Grasslands and savannahs
  • Latin name: Leo

Lions are muscular, deep-chested felids with short, rounded heads, reduced necks and round ears.[59] Of the living, non-hybrid felids, the lion is second only to the tiger in length and weight. Its skull is very similar to that of the tiger, although the frontal region is usually more depressed and flattened, with a slightly shorter postorbital region and broader nasal openings than that of a tiger.
